Mission :

The University of Warsaw Data Science Club was founded in 2018 and has since then brought together students who are true Data Science enthusiasts.

What we do :

Our members, despite their young age and seniority, stand out with their extensive knowledge in areas such as: Econometrics, Statistics, Machine Learning, Quantitative Finance, Microeconomics and Business Analysis. The mission of the KN Data Science UW is to implement academic and business projects in the area of widely understood Data Science, because we believe that only practical tasks allow us to constantly develop. We want to learn, build experience and have fun with data. Importantly, our circle is international. We bring together students from all over the world! In addition, we receive real scientific support (during problem solving seasons) from our mentors, which is why the products we provide are of the highest possible quality! As part of our expanded activity, we organize open meetings for students of the Faculty of Economic Sciences, University of Warsaw with world-class experts in the field of Data Science.

Join Us!

KN Data Science UW recruits all year round. If you want to join us, write to us via Linkedin or call us. (See Contact section) We approach each candidate individually, which is why we do not have a predefined recruitment path. We expect you to be enthusiastic, open minded and enjoy working!
